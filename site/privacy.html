Privacy Policy

Whiskerwatch is an offline-first app. All entries you log are stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, analytics, or usage information. There are no third-party trackers or advertising SDKs in this app.

Purchases (subscriptions or one-time unlocks) are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We never see or store your payment information.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed from your device.
